VFMADD132SS (XMM, XMM, XMM)	4	0.50 / 0.50	1 / 1	1*p01
VADDSS      (XMM, XMM, XMM)	2	0.50 / 0.50	1 / 1	1*p15
-> 2 FMAs on port 0 and 1 = 4 FLOPs
-> 1 ADD on port 5        = 1 FLOPs
= 5 FLOPs


VFMADD132PS (YMM, YMM, YMM)	4	0.50 / 0.50	1 / 1	1*p01
VADDPS      (YMM, YMM, YMM)	2	0.50 / 0.50	1 / 1	1*p15
-> 2 FMAs on port 0 and 1 * 8 f32s per instr = 32 FLOPs
-> 1 ADD on port 5        * 8 f32s per instr  = 8 FLOPs
= 40 FLOPs
